About ChatMinister

ChatMinister is a demo AI receptionist designed for clinics and healthcare front desks. It helps patients ask common questions, check clinic information, and get guided toward the right next step before speaking with staff.

Clinic FAQs

Answers questions about clinic hours, location, services, and consultation fees.

Appointment Guidance

Guides patients on what details are needed when requesting an appointment.

Doctor Availability

Shares listed doctor schedules and directs users to staff when confirmation is needed.

Safe Escalation

Refers urgent concerns and medical-advice questions to clinic staff or emergency services.

Important Notice

ChatMinister is a front-desk assistant only. It does not provide diagnosis, prescriptions, or emergency medical advice. For urgent concerns, please contact clinic staff or emergency services immediately.
